ABOUT ME

Hi, I’m Alexandros Giorginis, a 21-year-old student from Greece and Czechia. I’m currently studying Applied Computer Science at Thomas More in Geel.

I speak four languages: Greek, Czech, French, and English, which helps me connect with diverse perspectives. My passion for technology started young. I was always tinkering with electronics, so pursuing Computer Science felt like the perfect fit.

I discovered Thomas More during an open day, and the program’s focus on practical skills and innovation drew me in, leading me to apply. Beyond academics, I’m passionate about fitness and sports: I regularly hit the gym and play football as a goalkeeper for ASV Geel. I also enjoy designing and building websites, which lets me blend creativity with technical skills.

Looking ahead, I dream of becoming a professional goalkeeper while also making an impact in tech. I’d love to create innovative web applications and explore the potential of AI in Computer Science, perhaps developing intelligent systems that solve real-world problems.
